Frequently Asked Questions

What is the "No Lock-In Guarantee"?

Your website is 100% yours to keep forever. We provide you with all source files, and you can move to any hosting provider at any time. No contracts, no recurring fees, no hidden dependencies.

Do I need to pay upfront?

No prepayment required. You only pay after reviewing and approving your completed website. We believe in earning your trust through quality work, not contracts.

How long does it take?

Most websites are completed within 3-7 business days. You'll receive regular updates throughout the process and can request changes during your free revision period.

What if I don't like the website?

We include free revisions for 30-90 days (depending on package). If you're still not satisfied, you don't pay anything. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Can I update content myself?

Yes! We provide you with everything needed to update your website. Plus, we include basic training on how to make content changes yourself.

Do you provide hosting?

We can recommend reliable hosting providers, but you choose where to host. This ensures you maintain full control and ownership of your website and data.
